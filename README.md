## Picnic Panic

Simple mobile arcade-style game where the goal is to maneuver falling fruit to their respective picnic basket.

<img width="430" height="932" alt="iPhone 14   15 Pro Max - 5" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/da4872ff-eb6c-413a-89a2-486c566b3835" />

Player must tap on the arrows to guide the falling fruit to the picnic basket until they fail to do so. Difficulty increases by spawning fruits faster, and having them fall faster.

Created in 1 month as part of a learning experience. Purpose was to create a game that so intuitive, that you could understand how the game works based on a single screenshot.
